Our SOlutions

Offer Business Solutions Through Cutting-Edge solutions

Company Registration

Seamlessly register your Private Limited Company (PLC) or Private Business Corporation (PBC) in Zimbabwe. We handle all paperwork and liaise with the Companies Office for you.

  • Name search & reservation
  • Preparation of registration documents
  • Submission to Companies Office
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Company documents (CR6, CR14, Memorandum & Articles)

Tax Advisory & Management (ZIMRA)

Navigate Zimbabwean tax laws with ease. We provide expert advice, assist with ZIMRA registrations, and manage your tax affairs for optimal compliance and savings.

  • ZIMRA registration (BPN, VAT, PAYE, Income Tax)
  • Tax clearance certificate assistance
  • Tax planning & advisory
  • Filing of tax returns

Business Advisory solutions

Get expert guidance on structuring your business and ensuring full compliance. We offer tailored advice to help you make informed decisions for your venture in Zimbabwe.

  • Business structure advice
  • Regulatory compliance guidance
  • Business process optimization
  • Ongoing support & consultation

Trust Registration

Establish trusts for various purposes, including family, charitable, or business trusts. We guide you through the legal requirements for secure trust formation in Zimbabwe.

  • Drafting trust deed
  • Submission to Deeds Office
  • Obtaining trust registration certificate
  • Legal compliance guidance

ZIMRA Registration & Renewals

Ensure your business is fully registered with ZIMRA for all necessary tax heads. We also manage your annual renewals to keep you compliant and operational.

  • Initial ZIMRA registration
  • Annual tax renewals
  • Updating company tax records
  • Renewal reminders & support

NSSA Registration

Ensure your business complies with social security regulations. We manage your NSSA registration and ongoing contributions, simplifying your employment obligations.

  • NSSA employer registration
  • Employee registration
  • Monthly/quarterly returns
  • Compliance advisory

Company Profile Creation

Develop a professional company profile that effectively communicates your business's vision, mission, and services. Essential for tenders and corporate representation.

  • Profile content drafting
  • Professional layout & design
  • Branding integration
  • Editable & print-ready files

Logo & Letterhead Creation

Establish your brand identity with professional logo design and consistent letterheads. Essential for business credibility and official communication.

  • Custom logo design
  • Letterhead template creation
  • Brand color & font selection
  • Digital & print-ready formats
